[[Continuum Mechanics]]
## Covariant basis
### Cartesian reference frames

$$
\mathbf{x} = x^1 \mathbf{e}_1 + x^2 \mathbf{e}_2 + x^3 \mathbf{e}_3  = x^i \mathbf{e}_i
$$
$$
\mathbf{e}_i * \mathbf{e}_j = g_{ij} = \delta_{ij}
$$
### Arbitrary skew angled reference frames

$$
\mathbf{g}_i = \beta_{i}{}^{jยบ} \mathbf{e}_j
$$

The dot products of the base vectors of the skew angled covariant basis result in covariant **metric coefficients**:
$$
g_{ij} = \mathbf{g}_i * \mathbf{g}_j
$$

## Contravariant basis

$$
\mathbf{x} = x^i \mathbf{g}_i
$$

$$
\mathbf{g}_i * \mathbf{g}^k = \delta_{i}{}^{k}
$$
