## Equilibrium

$$
\sigma^{im}|_i + Q^m - \rho b^m = 0
$$

## Strains

$$
\sigma_{ij} = \frac{1}{2} (g_{ij} - G_{ij})
$$

## Elasticity tensor

$$
C_{ijkl} = \lambda G^{ij} G^{kl} + \mu (G^{ik} G^{kl} G^{jk})
$$

$$
\lambda = \frac{E \nu}{(1 + \nu)(1 - 2\nu)}
\quad \quad
\mu = G = \frac{E}{2(1 + \nu)}
$$
