[[Continuum Mechanics]]
## Line differentials

$$
\text{d}s_1 = \sqrt{g_{11}} \, \text{d}\theta^1
$$

## Surface differentials

$$
\begin{align}
\text{d} \mathbf{A}_1 
&= \mathbf{g}_2 \times \mathbf{g}_3 \text{d}\theta^2 \text{d}\theta^3
\\
&= \sqrt{g} \, \mathbf{g}^1 \text{d}\theta^2 \text{d}\theta^3
\\
&= | \mathbf{J} | \mathbf{g}^1 \text{d}\theta^2 \text{d}\theta^3
\end{align}
$$
## Volume differentials

$$
\text{d}V = | \mathbf{J} | \, \text{d} \theta^1 \text{d} \theta^2 \text{d} \theta^3
$$
